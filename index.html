ABUOT US

DELICIOUS FOOD is an elegant and informal fine dining restaurant with an emphasis on natural, sustainable food, sourced from the UK’s most dedicated farmers and food producers. We create beautiful craft dishes, seeking out the best of British produce, with a passion to delight and share our curiosity and our love of delicious artisanal food. Since opening in August 2017,
DELICIOUS FOOD has received numerous awards including two stars as new entry in the 2019 Michelin Guide, 10/10 in the Good Food Guide, five AA rosettes, Best Restaurant at the GQ Food and Drink Awards, New Restaurant of the Year by The Craft Guild of Chefs, and the Service Award at the National Restaurant Awards. Clare also received the World’s Best Female Chef Award by the World’s 50 Best Restaurants.
